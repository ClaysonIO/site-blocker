Privacy Policy

No Data Collection

The Site Blocker extension does not collect any personal information from users. All data related to your blocked sites is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to any external servers.

Local Storage Only

Your list of blocked websites and settings are stored exclusively in your browser's local storage. This information never leaves your device and is not accessible to anyone but you.

No Tracking

Site Blocker does not track your browsing history, behavior, or any other activity. The extension only checks if the current website matches any domain in your block list.

Permissions

The extension requires certain permissions to function properly, such as access to tabs and web navigation. These permissions are used solely for the purpose of blocking specified websites and are not used to collect or transmit any data.

Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy, please visit our GitHub repository.
